Welcome to Faith Chapel

At our core, we embody a church that embraces the profound love of God and extends that love to all. Our passion drives us to witness the divine through worship, facilitating personal encounters with God and fostering the liberating freedom found in knowing Jesus. Immerse yourself in fellowship with us and embrace the transformative power of His Word, Deliverance, and Healing.



OUR VISION



Cultivating a Nation that aspires and burns for the work of Christ, also inspiring everyone to love God, love others as ourselves and impact the world for Christ by spreading the word of God to all Nations.
